This is a story about a fox. The fox lived near a farm and he liked to wander onto sometimes. The farmer would always chase it away.



One day the fox met the farmers dog and they became best friends. The farmer stopped chasing the fox because he just wanted to play.

Everyday, the fox and the dog would go out exploring the nearby woods and fields. At the end of the day, they would come back to have dinner with the farmer.
